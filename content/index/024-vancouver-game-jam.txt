New Year, New Game, New Me

Another year around the sun, what an adventurous time. The new year always fills me with new ambition and this time that ambition took me to Vietnam! What a humbling experience, taking the Ha Giang Loop on motorcycle was so incredibly breathtaking. Meeting the locals and fellow tourist reminded me of what I need the most this year. I need to... STRUGGLE!

Yes you read that right, my new years goal is to struggle more. Apparently its apart of their culture, but viewing life as the moments where I struggle the most is a helpful framework and it really showed in how much the locals are doing day in and day out. The variety of people I met around the Hanoi and in the mountain villages stuck with me, how they live their life, how they spend their free time, how much energy they had. I will take a portion of that with me into my work and my life as well.

Speaking of work. I participated in the Vancouver Global Game Jam! My social skills were not up to cut unfortunately so I ended up starting the project solo. Thankfully an artist showed interest and joined in on my project. I built the Kirby Quick Draw game essentially for mobile in about a day and a half. Once again in Godot! I'm grateful that it worked out so well and is so easily demo-able. My learnings from my previous Godot Jam really carried me and I understand now the best thing I can do is aim to have something polished and easily playable. This portrait layout on mobile I think is a format I'd like to reproduce more.

Please check it out, its multiplayer but you can play vs yourself if you want <a href="https://snkz.itch.io/mask-off">Mask OFF</a>. I was able to reuse assets im building for my STILL IN DEVELOPEMENT tower defense game. Was nice to be try rendering those characters out in a new format.

With this, I've maintained my goal of producing a game a year. I'm hoping to do another game jam this year and I will do my best to build it with a team. It will be a <i>struggle</i> to work with others for me but I know its important, so I will try.
